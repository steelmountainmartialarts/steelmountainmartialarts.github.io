Welcome to





A personalized approach to stand-up martial arts in Ancaster.



At Steel Mountain Martial Arts, we offer a supportive, small-group environment dedicated to stand-up martial arts. Our core disciplines include American Kenpo Karate and Kickboxing, taught with a personal touch to students of all levels—from complete beginners to experienced martial artists.

Located in Ancaster, just behind Walmart, our dojo is a family-friendly space where growth, discipline, and fitness come together. With small class sizes—up to 12 adults or 15 children—you’ll receive focused instruction in a close-knit, respectful community.



Led by Experience.


Our head instructor began training in Kenpo Karate in the late 1980s and earned his black belt in 1993. With over 30 years of experience, including cross-training in multiple martial arts, he brings both technical depth and practical insight to every class.





Our Training Philosophy.


We emphasize clean technique, proper conditioning, and controlled application. Classes incorporate heavy bags, double-end bags, focus mitts, and pad work to develop timing, accuracy, and power. Sparring is done at 30%–50% contact, similar to the light-contact rules of WAKO Canada tournaments—ideal for learning real-world skills while minimizing injury.
