Welcome to


We Produce Gold Medal Winners



Welcome to Steel Mountain Martial Arts, we are a small personalized facility that specializes in stand up martial arts,our base martial art is Kenpo Karate, within the classes we also mix in elements of a few other martial arts such as kick boxing along with Wu Shu better known as Chinese Kung fu.

We pride ourselves on being a family friendly dojo, our classes consist of a maximum of twelve adults at one time or fifteen children. Our head instructor began training in Kenpo Karate back in the late 1980’s, after receiving his Black belt in 1993. He chose to dabble in a few other martial arts over the years for a better understanding of what different styles of martial arts can offer. Our classes have a strong focus on clean technique, along with a solid workout. We offer light to medium contact sparring, We are “Not” a full contact club. If you are interested in a full contact atmosphere we can recommend a few excellent clubs in the Hamilton area.



What sets us apart from the local dojo’s:


- Small group classes with a maximum of 15 children or 12 adults.

- We offer one of the best family pricing packages available in the area.

- We offer family classes, where you can come and spend time with your children and learn together.

- A structured environment where your children can develop self confidence and develop life long skills that will help them throughout their life.

- No monthly contract, if you are unhappy with our instruction or class environment you are free to leave at the end of the month.

- All new interests are welcome to try three free classes before making a personal commitment to learn within our studio.

- We offer you the ability to earn free training.



Thank you for your consideration, if you have any questions or would like to try a few classes please contact us.
