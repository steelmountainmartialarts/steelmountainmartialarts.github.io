Why Steel Mountain?

Steel Mountain Martial Arts is a family orientated martial arts facility with a strong belief in family and friends, originally the facility was only open to family and friends, over time we opened to everyone. While still maintaining our commitment to keeping the training very personal, we have limited the classes to fifteen children or twelve adults per class.

We offer family classes that contain children as young as eight years old with parents in their forties. Our Adult classes are designed for students with an interest in improving their flexibility and conditioning while learning the martial arts. Safety and respect for each other are main principles in our club.

One of our goals is to make martial arts accessible to everyone, you will find that we offer some of the best family rates in the area, by only charging $165.00 dollars per month for the whole family, provided they live in the same household. Review our price list below for our other rates.

Where did the name come from?

Steel Mountain Martial Arts was founded in Hamilton, Ontario. Hamilton contains some of the largest steel manufacturers in Ontario that have been employing thousands of people over multiple generations.

The city of Hamilton is on an escarpment that contains some of the most beautiful waterfalls in the area. Some people would say we are located on top of the mountain.

The head instructors' four children worked together along with a couple of their friends to come up with the name. Once the name was decided upon, the head instructor's youngest son then sat down and designed a logo based on the name.
